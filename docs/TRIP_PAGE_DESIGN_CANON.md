# Travel trip-page design canon v1

The canonical page uses an editorial travel-deal composition: large image gallery, concise emotional proposition, visible trip facts, date variants, detailed route, day-by-day cards, accommodation, inclusions, realistic total and evidence. It follows the information rhythm of modern package-tour pages without copying branding, proprietary assets, copy or exact CSS.

## Mandatory first screen

- destination and theme;
- exact dates with weekdays;
- days and nights;
- required working days;
- full total for two as the primary price;
- per-person price as secondary;
- page mode: candidate, planned or in-trip;
- one clear primary action.

## Section order

Gallery → proposition → date variants → why this trip → route → itinerary → stays → included/excluded → full cost → documents/risks → sources.

## Mobile rules

- mobile-first from 360 px;
- no horizontal page overflow;
- touch targets at least 44 px;
- horizontally scrollable date variants;
- sticky bottom summary instead of desktop price card;
- no embedded maps; use large Google Maps buttons.

## Content rules

- total for two includes realistic extras;
- evidence labels are explicit;
- candidate pages never imply booking;
- no false urgency or sales countdowns;
- private data never enters the public template repository;
- destination-side route links omit Brandenburg–BER legs.
